🧘 Personal: assistant of the day

This is the case of lowest risk andhigher perceived return. The agent becomes useful in 1 day. Daily summary at 8am, contextual reminders, quick searches via Telegram.

📋 Typical Crons and skills

  • →Chron 8h: "Send me a summary of the day's priorities"
  • →Chron 18h: "Review what remains open and propose 3 actions for tomorrow"
  • →Cron Fri 5pm: "Weekly report: progress, pending issues, next steps"
  • →Skill: quick-research— searches for a topic and returns 5 bullets

💡Why start here

Short crons, all via Telegram, without local GPU. You create a habit of talking to the agent every day — which makes feedback (and learning) constant.

2

📣 Marketing: monitor and respond

Cron monitoring comments (YouTube, Instagram, X),suggesting responses within the brand tone, maintaining a live editorial calendar. You become a reviewer, not a creator from scratch.

🎙️ What is VERY important here

  • SOUL.mdsets the tone of the brand — direct, relaxed, technical, etc.
  • MEMORY.mdsave examples of good posts and brand catchphrases
  • Skills: responder-comentario, sugerir-thread, brief-do-mes
  • Permissions: never give "always allow" to publish on networks — just suggest
3

💰 Financial: recurring reports

Cron reading spreadsheets, bank or financial API, generatingweekly summary, alerting you about due dates and cost anomalies.

✓ Recommended pattern

  • ✓Financial agentseparateof the staff
  • ✓API keys with read-only scope
  • ✓Alerts on Telegram channelprivate
  • ✓Audit logs of everything he did

✗ Never do

  • ✗Same key as personal agent
  • ✗Transfer permission active by default
  • ✗Direct response in a collective group
  • ✗"Always allow" in any financial action
4

🎧 Support: Message screening

Hermes reading email/tickets, sorting,suggesting answerbased on FAQ or customer history, escalating whichever is critical. High volume of repeated messages saves the most time.

🛠️ Typical support agent stack

  • • Soul: empathetic, professional, with escalation rules
  • • memory: Updated FAQ, doc links, owner contacts
  • • Skills: classificar-ticket, buscar-na-faq, escalar-para-humano
  • • Cron: every 30 minutes, sweep the cash register and sort new tickets
  • • Rule of thumb: never responds to the customer alone in the first 4 weeks — only suggests a response for you to review
5

⚙️ Operations: Server Health

Weekly audit cron:open ports, suspicious processes, disk space, abnormal logs. Automatic daily backup. You don't forget to keep the environment healthy.

🛡️ Useful Operation Crons

  • Daily Cron 00h: full directory backup~/.hermesfor private GitHub
  • Cron every 5 min(mode--no-agent): heartbeat of RAM, disk, process
  • Weekly Cron Sun 9am: auditing open ports and strange logs
  • Monthly Cron: review which skills you used, which ones you never used, and propose cleaning

💡Combine with external firewall

Audit skills do not replace host-layer firewall (ufw, Hostinger Upguard). Use both — layered security is the rule.

6

✍️ Content: ideation and review

Skills to revise text in your style, generate variations for A/B, suggest topics based on past content, create a weekly newsletter.Reduces “blank page” friction.

🧠 Well-made content = well-made soul + memory

The review skill completely depends on theSOUL.md(your voice) and theMEMORY.md(examples of your texts that worked). Without them, the agent homogenizes the text and takes away its personality.

Good phrase for him:"Revise keeping my voice. Fix clarity and structure. But if you take away my personality, worse."

📰 Classic newsletter cron

"Every Thursday at 2pm, read my Linear and my posts for the week, and prepare a draft newsletter in format X. I review it by 6pm and send it."

🎯Module summary

✓
Personnel is the entry point— low risk, return in 1 day, creates a habit.
✓
Marketing = soul + memory— brand tone is everything, agent suggests, you publish.
✓
Financial = separate agent— dedicated keys, read scope, private channel.
✓
Support = never responds alone at first— only suggests for 4 weeks.
✓
Operations = backup + audit + heartbeat— combine with external firewall.
✓
Content = preserve your voice—explicitly instruct “don’t homogenize me.”
